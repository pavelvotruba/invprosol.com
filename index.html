Industry Solution: Licensed Cannabis (Canada)

🌿 Ready-Made Customization for Cannabis

We offer a specific Ecount ERP configuration built for Canadian licensed cannabis companies with cultivation and processing licences. It’s already refined in production with a client live since June 1, 2025.

  • Seed-to-sale traceability: genetics/strain, plant/room tracking, harvest lots, curing/drying, packaging.
  • Inventory, production, sales, and procurement modules fully integrated with financials for one end-to-end system.
  • Lot/batch inventory with full forward/backward trace.
  • Production BOMs & yields for oil, pre-rolls, extracts, edibles, topicals.
  • Quality events (deviations/CAPA), holds/release status.
  • Excise-relevant data capture; cost layers and duties accounted in COGS.
  • Role-based access and auditable change history.

📑 Reports & Compliance Support

Templates and data structures aligned to typical Health Canada reporting expectations (e.g., CTLS monthly reporting inputs) and common LP operational KPIs.

  • CTLS-friendly data exports (inputs for monthly submissions).
  • Inventory by lot/phase, destruction logs, packaging/label claims data.
  • COA linking and QA release tracking.
  • Sales/returns by SKU & potency ranges.

Note: We support your compliance processes, but ultimate compliance responsibility remains with your business. Always review Health Canada guidance and your legal counsel’s advice.

✅ Live Since June 1, 2025

A Canadian licensed processor has been operating on this configuration since June 1, 2025, covering purchasing, production, packaging, QA, and distribution.

📂 Historical Data Migration

We are migrating all historical transactions for 2025 (starting January 1, 2025) into our ERP solution, so that financial reporting can be performed from one integrated system covering operations and financials.

⭐ Key Benefits

  • End-to-end integration across cultivation, processing, and finance.
  • Automated compliance reporting support (CTLS inputs, COA linking, QA release).
  • Custom solutions for cannabis operations (enhanced dashboards in development using PostgreSQL).
  • Reliable technology stack: Ecount ERP + PostgreSQL.
